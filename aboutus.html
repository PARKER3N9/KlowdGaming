Get to know Klowd Gaming

Klowd Gaming is a group of friends who came together through video games. I, CP3 K9, know as Christian Parker, wanted to create a place where people could play, both competitively and casually and have fun. I developed a love for video games just after high school when I fell in love with League Of Legends. After seriving a religous mission, I still loved the game, and having grown up playing sports, wanted somewhere I could learn and compete at the game. I found and was part of multiple orginizations, but none really stuck with me, so I made my own! This is a family we have built and want to continue to grow and expand, giving other the chance to play and learn in their respective video games. We provide coaching, payment for leagues and tournaments and overall strive to provide everything our players need to be successful.
